Bars

The Owl Bar

East Chase St., Mid-Town Belvedere, Baltimore, MD 21201 (410) 347-0888

It's days as a speakeasy have been over for decards, but the bar located on the Belvedere Hotel's first floor retains a sense of history and mystery that's easy to feel but difficult to accurately descibe. Experience it yourself with a pint and a pizza.

1928 Fleet St., Fells Point, Baltimore, MD 21224 (410) 979-5161

Famous for it's Bacon Night, this Fells Point corner dive bar has a secret weapon not made of pork: Owner John Reusing, who remains one of the city's best bartenders thanks to delicious and out-of-the-box cocktails.

Of Love and Regret

View Photo

View Photo

1028 South Conkling St., Canton, Baltimore, MD 21224 (410) 327-0760

Part-bar, part-restaurant, part-lounge and part-bottle shop, Of Love and Regret is a lot of things. Most of all, it's famed brewer Brian Strumke's first brick-and-mortar location for Stillwater Ales and a fantastic place to try esoteric beers.
