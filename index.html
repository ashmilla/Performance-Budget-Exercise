Bars

The Owl Bar

East Chase St., Mid-Town Belvedere, Baltimore, MD 21201 (410) 347-0888

It's days as a speakeasy have been over for decades, but the bar located on the Belvedere Hotel's first floor retains a sense of history and mystery that's easy to feel but difficult to accurately descibe. Experience it yourself with a pint and a pizza.

Of Love and Regret

View Photo

View Photo

1028 South Conkling St., Canton, Baltimore, MD 21224 (410) 327-0760

Part-bar, part-restaurant, part-lounge and part-bottle shop, Of Love and Regret is so many things. But most of all, it's famed brewer Brian Strumke's first brick-and-mortar location for Stillwater Ales and a fantastic place to try some esoteric beers.
